    About Human Act Human Act is a worldwide development organization that aims to make a difference in the life of the millions of people living in extreme poverty, guided by love, nonviolence, purpose, humility, courage, generosity, empathy, forgiveness, joy, and transparency. Move Humanity is Human Act’s flagship campaign and aims to mobilize an additional $91 billion toward the implementation of the SDGs by advocating for an increase in private development assistance (PDA) from the world's wealthiest individuals.
      For more information, follow #MoveHumanity on Twitter or visit: Move Humanity
